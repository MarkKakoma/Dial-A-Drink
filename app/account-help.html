Frequently asked
How long does delivery take?⌄

Most orders arrive within 30–45 minutes inside Harare. You can also schedule a delivery window for later in the day at checkout.

What areas do you deliver to?⌄

We deliver across Harare and surrounding suburbs during opening hours. Enter your address at checkout to confirm we reach you.

How does store credit work?⌄

Change from cash orders and promotions are added to your store credit. It can be spent on any future order with Dial A Drink, but can't be transferred or withdrawn as cash.

Can I pay cash on delivery?⌄

Yes. Choose "Cash on delivery" at checkout. Your driver will bring change, and any change you choose to leave can be added to your store credit.

Do I need to be 18 to order?⌄

Yes. Alcohol is not for sale to persons under the age of 18. Our drivers may ask for ID on delivery. Please drink responsibly.
